About Hallie

Hallie is a 19 year old American currently spending her first year out of high school in Europe au pairing for an amazing German family. Hallie's hobbies include visiting and experiencing the beauty of nature and architecture, diving into different cultures, and watching Gilmore Girls for the umpteenth time. This blog is her story and her experience exploring and having the best time of her life in Europe.
